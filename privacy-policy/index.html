1. Overview

SnapSolve AI ("we," "our," or "the App") is a mobile application that provides AI-powered screen diagnostics and troubleshooting. Users can capture or upload screenshots of technical issues and receive instant, step-by-step solutions generated by artificial intelligence.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our application. By using SnapSolve AI, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

🛡️ We are committed to transparency. We do not sell your personal data to third parties, and we minimize data collection to only what is necessary for the app to function.

📊

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of information required to provide our services:

Data Type Purpose Storage
Email Address Account creation and authentication Local device
Screenshots/Photos AI analysis and troubleshooting Temporary (processing only)
Analysis History Review past solutions Local device only
App Preferences Language, theme, settings Local device only

We do not collect: GPS location, contacts, call logs, browsing history, financial information, or biometric data.

📷

3. Camera & Media Access

SnapSolve AI requests access to your device camera and photo gallery for the following purposes:

  • Camera Permission: To capture live photos of screens, error messages, or technical issues for real-time AI analysis.
  • Gallery/Media Access: To allow you to upload existing screenshots from your device for analysis.

📷 Camera and gallery access is used exclusively for capturing images to analyze. We do not access your camera or photos in the background, and we do not store your images on any external server after processing.

You can revoke camera and storage permissions at any time through your device's Settings → Apps → SnapSolve AI → Permissions.

🌐

4. Internet Usage

SnapSolve AI requires an active internet connection for the following purposes:

  • AI Analysis: Sending captured images to Google's AI servers for processing and receiving diagnostic results.
  • Font Loading: Loading custom typography from Google Fonts for the user interface.
  • Updates: Checking for app updates and security patches.

All network communication is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols. The app does not permit cleartext (unencrypted) HTTP traffic. We enforce strict network security policies to protect data in transit.

🤖

5. Google Gemini AI Processing

SnapSolve AI uses Google Gemini AI (specifically the Gemini 2.5 Flash model) to analyze screenshots and generate troubleshooting solutions. Here is how your data is handled during AI processing:

  • Image Transmission: When you submit a screenshot for analysis, the image is sent securely to Google's Gemini API servers for processing.
  • Processing: The AI model analyzes the visual content of your screenshot to identify errors, issues, or problems, and generates step-by-step solutions.
  • Temporary Processing: Images are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on Google's servers beyond the processing session.
  • No Training: Your submitted images are not used to train or improve Google's AI models when accessed through the API.

🤖 AI processing is governed by Google's Gemini API Terms of Service. We encourage you to review their data handling practices.

🔐

6. Authentication & Login

SnapSolve AI offers optional user authentication:

  • Guest Mode: You can use the app without creating an account. No personal information is required.
  • Email Registration: You may optionally create an account using your email address and a password.
  • Password Security: Passwords are securely hashed and stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. We never store plaintext passwords.
  • Session Management: Login sessions are managed locally on your device.

We do not use third-party social login providers (Google Sign-In, Facebook Login, etc.) at this time. Your authentication data remains on your device.

💾

7. Data Handling & Storage

We follow a local-first approach to data storage:

  • Analysis History: All past analysis results are stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. This data never leaves your device.
  • User Preferences: Theme settings, language preferences, and onboarding state are stored locally using secure shared preferences.
  • Sensitive Data: Authentication tokens and credentials are stored using platform-specific encrypted storage (Android Keystore).
  • Data Deletion: You can clear all analysis history from within the app's Settings screen. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

💾 Your analysis history, preferences, and account data are stored exclusively on your device. We do not maintain external databases of user data.

9. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted Communication: All API calls use HTTPS/TLS encryption. Cleartext HTTP traffic is explicitly blocked.
  • Network Security Config: The app enforces a strict Network Security Configuration that only permits secure HTTPS connections.
  • Code Obfuscation: Release builds use ProGuard/R8 obfuscation to protect against reverse engineering.
  • Secure Storage: Sensitive data is stored using platform-level encrypted storage mechanisms.
  • API Key Protection: API keys are injected at build time and never exposed in the source code or local storage.
  • Backup Disabled: Android auto-backup is disabled to prevent sensitive data from being backed up to cloud services.

While we take every reasonable precaution to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
